Services

Details of some of the services we provide

Vehicle tracking system

It enables you to track your fleet directly with the ability to return to previous trips. Alerts are also available when moving and when entering and leaving a specific area that the customer can specify. A service is also available to calculate engine oil changes for all cars in the fleet.

Call system

Employees can make calls and communicate with each other or with clients from inside the office and outside the office wherever the employee is present. An application is also available for mobile and computer to export and import calls with the ability to record calls and return to them at any time. An unlimited number of users can also be created with The ability to create more than one supervisor to be familiar with specific user interfaces only

accounts management system

A specialized accounting system that allows the company to create invoices, price quotes, subscriptions, and other matters related to accounting

Human Resources System

A system that allows the company to register all employees, calculate attendance and departure, and calculate absence. An unlimited number of users can also be created, with many other benefits available
